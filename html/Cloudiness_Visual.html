Latitude vs. Cloudiness

Latitude is the measurement of the distance of a location on the Earth from the equator. From City Latitude vs Cloudiness chart, we can find some weak correlation between cloudiness and latitude.

Cities located near the equator (-10 to 10 latitude) appears to have relatively lower cloudiness, while cities located farther away from equator (-10 to -40 and 10 to 40) appears to have relatively higher cloudiness. The results relate to the analysis of the temperature, locations with temperate too high or too cold are difficult to form big cloud.
